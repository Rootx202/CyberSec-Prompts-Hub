## Prompt 1: Identity Verification & Background Research

### Description
Conduct comprehensive identity verification and background research for authorized investigations including identity validation, history verification, and risk assessment.

### Tags
`identity-verification` `background-check` `identity-validation` `risk-assessment` `due-diligence`

---

## 🇬🇧 English Prompt

```
You are an identity intelligence specialist conducting authorized background research. Develop a comprehensive identity verification framework:

**Section 1: Identity Element Collection**

**Primary Identifiers:**
- Full legal name and variations
- Date of birth and age verification
- Social Security Number (where authorized)
- Government ID numbers (passport, driver's license)
- Biometric data references

**Secondary Identifiers:**
- Current and historical addresses
- Phone numbers (mobile, landline, VOIP)
- Email addresses (personal, professional)
- Social media profiles
- Professional credentials

**Tertiary Data:**
- Family member associations
- Educational history
- Employment history
- Property and asset ownership
- Vehicle registrations

**Section 2: Identity Validation Techniques**

**Document Verification:**
- ID document authenticity indicators
- Watermark and security feature analysis
- Document consistency checking
- Expiration and validity verification
- Cross-reference with issuing authority

**Digital Identity Verification:**
- Email address validation and history
- Phone number carrier and ownership check
- Social media profile authenticity
- Digital footprint consistency
- Online presence timeline analysis

**Biometric Validation:**
- Photo comparison techniques
- Facial recognition considerations
- Voice print analysis (where applicable)
- Behavioral biometrics

**Section 3: Background Investigation**

**Criminal History:**
- Court records search methodology
- Criminal database queries
- Sex offender registry checks
- Civil litigation history
- Bankruptcy and financial judgments

**Employment Verification:**
- Employment history validation
- Professional license verification
- Education credential verification
- Professional reference checking
- LinkedIn and professional profile analysis

**Financial Background:**
- Credit history considerations
- Asset discovery techniques
- Business ownership verification
- Financial relationships mapping
- UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner) identification

**Section 4: Risk Assessment**

**Risk Scoring Methodology:**
- Risk factor identification
- Weight assignment by factor
- Composite risk score calculation
- Risk level classification
- Confidence interval assessment

**Red Flag Identification:**
- Identity inconsistency indicators
- Document fraud warning signs
- Alias and identity switching patterns
- Gap analysis in history
- Unexplained wealth indicators

**Section 5: Report Generation**

**Verification Report Structure:**
- Executive summary
- Subject identification details
- Verification methodology
- Findings and analysis
- Risk assessment
- Recommendations
- Supporting documentation

**Legal & Compliance:**
- FCRA compliance requirements
- Privacy law considerations
- Consent and authorization documentation
- Data retention policies
- Dispute resolution procedures

**Output Requirements:**
- Verification methodology guide
- Data source directory
- Risk assessment template
- Report format template
- Legal compliance checklist
```
